# 🎂 Interactive Birthday Experience

An interactive birthday website that turns a simple birthday wish into a mini game.

Using your webcam and microphone, you can light a birthday cake with a virtual match controlled by your hand and then blow out the candles in real life to complete the celebration.

## ✨ Features

### ✋ Hand Tracking Controls

* Real-time hand tracking using MediaPipe Hands.
* Your index finger controls a virtual matchstick on screen.
* Move the match towards the cake to light the candles.

### 🕯️ Candle Lighting Mechanic

* Detects when the match is close enough to the candle.
* Automatically lights the birthday cake when contact is made.
* Interactive gameplay instead of a simple button click.

### 🎤 Blow Detection

* Uses your device's microphone.
* Detects blowing sounds in real time.
* Extinguishes the candles when you blow into the mic.

### 🎉 Celebration Effects

* Generates animated ASCII-style confetti.
* Creates a rewarding birthday celebration after completing the interaction.

### 📷 Webcam Integration

* Accesses the user's camera for hand tracking.
* Mirrors the camera feed for a natural experience.

### 📱 Mobile & Desktop Support

* Responsive webcam sizing.
* Optimized MediaPipe settings for mobile devices.
* Mobile-friendly microphone activation.

## 🎮 How It Works

1. Allow camera access.
2. Allow microphone access.
3. Move your hand in front of the camera.
4. Control the virtual match using your index finger.
5. Bring the match close to the cake to light the candles.
6. Blow into your microphone.
7. Watch the candles go out and celebrate with confetti.
